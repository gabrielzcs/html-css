Box Level and Inline Level

Box level will use all the width of a page and will break lines automatic, both up and down.

Box level examples:

<div>

<h1-h6>

<main>

<header>

<nav>

<article>

<aside>

<footer>

<form>

<video>

Inline Level will only use the space to support whats inside of it and will not break line

Inline Level Examples:

<span>

<a>

<code>

<small>

<strong>

<em>

<sup-sub>

<label>

<button>

<input>

<select>

Shorthands for box border, padding, margin and outline:

border: border-width border-style border-color;

paddin: padding-top padding-right padding-bottom padding-left;

margin: margin-top margin-right margin-bottom margin-left;
